一 Chapter One

The Blue City
青城

Hohhot — 呼和浩特 — means "blue city" in Mongolian. Perched at the edge of the great grasslands where yellow loess farmland meets an infinite sky, it is a place of extremes: scorching summers and bone-white winters, ancient Silk Road echoes and quiet village mornings.

呼和浩特，蒙古语意为"青色的城"。坐落于大草原边缘，黄土农田与内蒙古辽阔天空交汇之处。这里气候极端：炎热的夏日与冰白的冬雪，古老的丝路回响与寂静的村间晨光。

二 Chapter Two

The Courtyard
院落

点击各处，探索这座院落的每个角落 · Click anywhere to explore

这不只是一栋房子——它是一座小广场，一个被四面墙围住的世界。正房朝南，捕捉每一缕冬日阳光。厢房里住着亲戚，储着谷物，积淀着几代人的记忆。

This was more than a house — it was a small plaza, an entire world within four walls. The main hall faced south, as tradition demanded, to capture every hour of winter sun. The wings housed family, stored grain, and held the memory of generations.
